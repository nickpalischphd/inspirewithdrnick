The Real Stars

Meet Dr. Nick's Corgi Family

Every character in the Mogwai & Friends series is inspired by a real, living, tail-wagging, zoomie-running, couch-hogging Corgi. These four are the heart behind every story.

Willow 👑

The Queen

Willow doesn't just rule the house — she rules it with grace, dignity, and an air that makes it very clear who is actually in charge. Regal, beautiful, and completely aware of it.

Mogwai 🌟

The Hero & Star

Curious, brave, and full of questions about the world. Mogwai is the lead character of the series — a little Corgi with a big heart and even bigger dreams.

Winston 😤❤️

The Grouch & The Lover

Winston will absolutely give you a look that says he wants nothing to do with you — right before he crawls into your lap and refuses to move. Grumpy on the outside, pure love on the inside.

Baxter ⚡

The Wild Card

Energetic, mischievous, and always getting into something. Baxter brings the laughs and reminds everyone that life is better with a little chaos and a lot of joy.

Always Remembered

In Loving Memory of Abby & Cooper

Two Corgis who left paw prints on Dr. Nick's heart that time can never erase. Their love, joy, and spirit live on in every story written and every page turned.

Abby 🐾

"She didn't just bring joy — she was joy. Every room she entered became brighter."

Cooper 🐾

"He taught us what unconditional love looks like. We are better people for having known him."

"What we lose shapes us, but it doesn't define us." — Dr. Nick
