Why You're Here

Everyone Has a Story

Sometimes we think our lives are too ordinary to matter. We assume our experiences are not big enough, dramatic enough, or important enough to inspire someone else. But the truth is, the stories that impact people the most are often the stories of everyday people who kept going, kept believing, kept learning, and kept showing up.


Your story may be about a life-changing event, a personal loss, a second chance, a career path, a hobby, or a quiet moment of faith. It may be something you have never thought of as a story at all. But it is — and it may be exactly what someone else needs to hear.

About

Meet Dr. Nick

PhD · Higher Education Leadership Executive Juris Doctorate Author Ordained Minister Meteorologist Public Safety Speaker Storyteller Corgi Dad

Dr. Nick holds a PhD in Higher Education Leadership and an Executive Juris Doctorate — credentials that reflect not just years of rigorous academic work, but a deep commitment to understanding people, systems, and how leadership shapes lives at every level.


He is a writer, author, speaker, meteorologist, public safety professional, ordained minister, and storyteller who believes deeply in the power of perspective. Through his writing, speaking, and community work, Dr. Nick helps people find meaning in everyday moments — exploring faith, personal growth, resilience, kindness, and the lessons life teaches when we slow down and listen.


Whether writing through Dr. Nick's Matter of Perspective, sharing weather insight through First Warn Weather, officiating life's most sacred ceremonies, or bringing joy through his children's book series Mogwai and Friends, Dr. Nick brings a thoughtful and honest voice rooted in compassion, humor, faith, and real-life experience.


At home, Dr. Nick shares his life with four beloved Corgis — Willow, Mogwai, Winston, and Baxter — who bring endless joy, laughter, and inspiration to his days. He also honors the memory of two very special Corgis who have passed: Abby and Cooper, who will always hold a place in his heart. 🐾

He brings warmth, thoughtfulness, and genuine care to every ceremony — treating each moment with the reverence and personal attention it deserves.

Perspective Changes Everything

"Life does not always give us easy answers. Sometimes we have to walk through uncertainty, loss, disappointment, and frustration before we understand what the lesson was supposed to be. But perspective can change how we see the road in front of us."
