I have heard a lot of people say some version of this: "My story isn't that interesting."

And every single time, they are wrong.

Not because every story is dramatic. Not because every story has a perfect ending. But because every story is real. And real is what connects us.

The most powerful stories are often the ones we almost didn't share. The quiet ones. The ones that feel too small or too personal or too unfinished.

Those are the ones that make someone else feel less alone.

Your story matters. Not in spite of its ordinariness — but because of it.
