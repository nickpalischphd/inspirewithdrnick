There are chapters in life that don't come with explanations. No footnotes. No clear reasons why. Just a season that demands more than you feel like you have to give.

I have walked through a few of those. You probably have too.

And here is what I have found — when life doesn't make sense, it doesn't mean something has gone wrong. Sometimes it means something is being formed. Something you can't see yet. Something that will only make sense on the other side.

Faith doesn't always remove the confusion. Sometimes it just gives you permission to walk through it without completely falling apart.

Keep going. The meaning is there, even when you can't find it yet.
