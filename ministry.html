Grief & Remembrance

Starting at $100

Saying goodbye is never easy. Dr. Nick offers compassionate, faith-filled memorial services that honor a life well-lived and bring comfort to those left behind. He takes time to know the person and their story.

  • Family consultation and tribute
  • Personalized eulogy guidance
  • Graveside or chapel services
  • Faith-centered or celebration of life

Baptisms

New Beginnings in Faith

Starting at $75
